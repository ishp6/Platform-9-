⚠ Concealment Notice
Walk with purpose toward the barrier between platforms 9 and 10. Do not run unless the barrier is crowded. Do not attempt to pass through if wearing obviously Muggle clothing without first consulting station staff. The platform is Fidelius-adjacent — Muggles perceive only a brick wall.

Luggage Policy
✦

✓ Permitted Items

  • One standard school trunk (max 150 litres)
  • One owl, cat, or toad per student
  • Approved spell books and class equipment
  • Brooms (stored in luggage van)
  • Telescopes and star charts
  • Cauldrons (pewter, size 2 standard)

✗ Prohibited Items

  • Dragon eggs or any live dragon species
  • Jinxed or cursed objects (undeclared)
  • Three-headed dogs or dangerous beasts
  • Muggle electronic devices
  • Unregistered animagi (must declare)
  • Dark Arts paraphernalia of any kind
